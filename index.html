Who I Am

BC-Based Municipal Planner
Ready for Consulting

I am a BC-based municipal planning professional with hands-on experience processing development applications, interpreting zoning and OCP policies, and supporting land use decision-making within BC local government.

Most recently as a Planning Technician at the Town of Ladysmith, I managed the full lifecycle of Development Permits, Development Variance Permits, and other development applications — from intake through referrals, staff reports, and Council presentations. I also managed Business Licences, Sign Permits, and Building Permit referrals.

Prior to Ladysmith, I worked as a Planning Consultant on Airport Master Plans and housing-focused Area Structure Plans, gaining broad exposure to policy analysis, bylaw amendments, and public engagement across British Columbia.

I hold a Master of Community Planning from VIU, am a Candidate Member of PIBC and CIP, and communicate fluently in English, Punjabi, and Hindi — an asset in diverse community engagement contexts.
